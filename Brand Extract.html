Construction

The mark reads as a stylized W built from three top stems and two right-leaning legs. A small horizontal gap between the stems and legs gives it the segmented, piano-key personality.

  • Top stems · three vertical bars, the third inset 21u to create the stepped notch on the right
  • Bottom legs · two parallelograms, vertical right edge, left edge slanting in
  • Wordmark · heavy geometric sans, wide tracking, all-caps
  • Descriptor · medium weight, 0.5em letter-spacing — never tighter
